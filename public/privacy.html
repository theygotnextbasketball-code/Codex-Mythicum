Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Codex Mythicum ("the App") is operated by Ivan Grishaev. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Codex Mythicum is designed to respect your privacy. The app stores preferences and subscription status locally on your device using browser local storage. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

- Subscription/premium status and license key
- Daily usage counters (archive views, algorithm rolls)
- API key (if provided by the user for AI features)
- App installation preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

Lemon Squeezy — for processing premium subscription payments. When you make a purchase, Lemon Squeezy collects payment information according to their own privacy policy: lemonsqueezy.com/privacy

Amazon Associates — the App contains affiliate links to books on Amazon. Clicking these links takes you to Amazon's website, which is governed by Amazon's privacy policy.

Anthropic API — if you use the AI creature generation feature with your own API key, your prompts are sent to Anthropic's API. See anthropic.com/privacy

Google Fonts — the App loads fonts from Google's servers. See Google's privacy policy

Cookies

The App does not use cookies. Local storage is used solely for app functionality as described above.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
